Born in France, Romain Axisa first picked up a guitar when he was 12, recording his first songs in a broken camper van in his parents' garden. In 2016, Romain moved to the UK with just his acoustic guitar and a bag full of clothes and began busking on the streets of Brighton, forming The Big Push with fellow buskers Ren Gill and Gorran Kendall. Returning once again to his solo career, his first single, ‘’It All Feels Old’’ has already amassed more than 100K streams and his new album will launch this summer.
